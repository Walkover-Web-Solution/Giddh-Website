In a developing country like India where businesses are thriving to keep up with the changing needs of the business, it is very important for it to be tech-savvy. Online accounting is a utility oriented service because it allows the business to go online, thereby making the business tech-savvy.

Online accounting is also a cost-effective method since you do not need to buy a license for using it and your information stays with you regardless of where your desktop is.
